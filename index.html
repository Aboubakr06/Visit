Cinema capitol

Ouarzazate has Atlas Studios, one of the largest movie studios in the world in terms of land area.Alot of Movies were shot here including The Kingdom of Heaven and Gladiator. It was also the location were Game of Thrones (Season 3) and Prison Break Season 5 was filmed.
